The Beginnings




Michael Jeffery Jordan was born on February 17th, 1963 in Brooklyn, New York. His family then moved to Wilmington, North Carolina when he was a small child. Jordan attended Emsley A. Laney High School, in his sophomore year; he tried out for the Varsity basketball team, but never made the team; he was classed as too short, at 5’11”, to make the team – Jordan was sad, because he felt he was better than some of the other players. Motivated by this failure he trained extra hard – he even grew 4 inches, and eventually made the team the next year. The flames in Jordan never quenched – as he went on to score a bunch of 40 points games.
